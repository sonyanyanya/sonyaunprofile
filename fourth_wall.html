Breaking the Fourth Wall Through my Writings

How many more words,
How many more days left?
To become the greater good
How many more letters
Do I have to write?
How many more songs
should I listen to ?
How many more things
Do I have to remember
And eventually forget,
to reach there?
There? where?
One may ask
'Who knows'
Answers the youth
Probably some place
warm and comforting
Is all one wishes for, but
Not me
I want it to be ice cold.
What will become of me?
Me a person so uninterested
& aimless with no ambition
Whatsoever.



Who finds peace in
The little things,
The bare minimum.
What will become of me?
Me who wants to only sip mediocre
coffee & write mediocre poems
For a mediocre audience.
What will become of me?
That aspires to sing aloud
but will do a 9to5 day job instead.
Tell me my fellow travellers,
What will become of us?
We are so close to reality
That its heartbreaking
Heartbreaking that it was all a lie
The magic, the magic
Where is it ?
Come find me won't you?
The sheer brilliance of it
will I come across it
Atleast once?
And when I do so will I know?....

PS- I want to be the kid that holds the mic
for the first time, about to tell a story again.



A fall where there is no landing
but only the fear of crashing onto the ground.
This is the time when the parrot is ready to leave
the diamond studded cage.
This is the inception of brutal individualism
which will cause mayhem for people around and oneself.
This chaotic state of mind might calm down one day
but there is no way to come out of it.


I like how the 3.45 pm sunlight falls
right on my bed,
the exact place where my head is.
I like how the honey-colored sunlight
paints the blue wall behind me,
leaving my perfect silhouette on it.
I like how the sunlight falls on my eyelashes and creates
these sparkly circles of light that make me feel picturesque.
I know I'd fall in love with myself
only if I could see me right now.
I mean, one can write poems,
but what to do when one becomes the poem itself.


Heating me up
With the reds, yellows, greens
And the occasional blues.
The pattern that keeps changing
From lines to checks,
From checks to lines
Are the lines of my veins
Completely visible
As you shine brightly, At me
So bright that I have
my eyes closed
And I see myself
Peeking within the universe in me.

Warming me real up
Bring me back to life
Giving me the breath
Giving me the moment needed.

Giving me the chance to become
vulnerable again,
Vulnerable again to life
 To what more is to come
To what I've been missing
To what I want to be
And to what I have become

Warming me real up
Like the flowers about to bloom
Like the younglings about to leave the nest
This metamorphosis is real
Is incomparable
Is exhilarating.

Cheers to all that exists today,
tomorrow and forever.
For the glass is half full
Full with dreams
Full with possibilities
Full with everything and nothing at all
All at once.
